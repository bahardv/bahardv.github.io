Bahar D Viniche, PhD

Hello and Welcome to My Website

I am a PostDoctoral Fellow in Operations Management at Rotman School of Management, University of Toronto working with Prof. Sheng Liu and Prof. Nooshin Salari. I work at the Intersection of Operations Research, Transportation, and Data-Deriven Optimization.

I earned my Ph.D. in Transportation Engineering and Management from York University, where I also served as a researcher in the Interactive-OR Lab. My PhD research focused on developing decision-support models for last-mile logistics, using advanced optimization techniques, parametric design, and machine learning algorithms. I had the privilege of working under the supervision of Prof. Mehdi Noruinejad, along with co-advisors Prof. Opher Baron and Prof. Oded Berman.

I am honored to have received the 2023 Women in Transportation WTS Toronto Chapter Award, recognizing my strong academic performance and my dedication to promoting equity, diversity, and inclusion (EDI).

Prior to beginning my Ph.D. studies, I obtained my MSc in Air Transportation from Sharif University of Technology, where I worked on a System Dynamics model for airline expansion. I also obtained my BSc in Aerospace Engineering from Sharif University of Technology.
